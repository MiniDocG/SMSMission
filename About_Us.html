About Us

Our Story

Save My Soul (SMS) Mission was placed on Susanna’s heart during her teenage years when she witnessed God use her parents daily to bring healing to people’s lives. Her parents ran an inner healing and deliverance ministry, called Greenhouse Ministries, where she witnessed over 500 people set free from trauma, demonic oppression, and addictions. Witnessing these transformations ignited a lifelong passion in her - to see people healed and restored by the power of God.

Throughout her teenage years and early twenties, God used Susanna to bring healing, freedom, and love to many people. However, it wasn’t until she was pursuing her undergraduate degree in Physics and Astronomy she would come to know the God who could heal her. After experiencing psychological and emotional abuse at the hands of a university professor, she was left dealing with emotional breakdowns, panic attacks, and rage issues. But God met her in that place and over the course of a three-year healing journey, He restored every part of her so today she lives completely free from this experience. 

It was during her PhD in Astrophysics, that God decided to bring healing from many other experiences including, childhood traumas, unfaithfulness in relationships, and much more. Each time, God walked through the pain with Susanna and restored her. Now, Susanna lives a healed life and she’s committed to helping others do the same. She wants to use her knowledge and experiences to show that anyone and everyone can live a healed life. 

It wasn’t until she began working as a Data Scientist at the Alan Turing Institute that God reignited the original dream placed in her heart - to start SMS Mission. SMS Mission is a movement where we want to see as many people healed from their pain so that they can go on to bring healing to those around them… because healed people, heal others. SMS Mission is a movement where people are given the tools needed to live a healed lifestyle and shown how to overcome any pain they will experience in the future. SMS is a movement where we want to show you how much you are loved by God and that He will go to any lengths to bring healing in your life. God is fighting for the real you and so are we.

The Summary

  • God is: Father, Son and Holy Spirit
  • You are: Soul, Body and Spirit
  • SMS Mission: Inner healing, discipleship and deliverance

In the beginning, God created everything and everyone perfectly. God created mankind in his own image, which means we are triune beings. Humans have a soul (will, mind and emotions), body, and spirit (God-given and how God communicates with humans). However, this perfection was soon tainted as sin entered the human heart. What is sin? God built mankind on a moral framework, like Physics is built on a mathematical framework. Sin is the word given to describe the action of a human heart breaking this moral framework. Whether one believes in this moral framework is irrelevant, the consequences are still the same. Similarly, it’s irrelevant whether you believe in Physics (or not), the Earth still orbits around the Sun. Humans thought they did not need God and that sin was a figment of their imagination.

So, what are the consequences of sin? Sin causes the human heart to break, in other words, a part of you dies. The human heart will break whether you commit a sin or the sin is committed to you. This means that a part of you comes away from the real you (i.e. mini souls come away from the actual soul) and you lose a part of yourself. Sin also gives permission for demons to enter the human body. Demons, like their father Satan, hate humans and their aim is to kill, steal and destroy humans. Once a demon is inside a human, they kill your enjoyment, steal your peace, and destroy your life from the inside out. 

God the Father, loves His creation, especially humans, but could see the damage they were causing themselves and others. So He decided to send Jesus, His Son, to die on the cross and take on the consequences of sin. This means that through Jesus, humans can have a whole heart, be free of demonic influence, and train themselves to have life-giving habits. This is why at SMS mission we are focused on providing resources and experience on inner healing, deliverance and discipleship so that you can live completely free of the consequences of sin. Through Jesus, you can have the final say… not sin!
