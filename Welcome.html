Welcome

Our Vision

We want to see over a million people healed by God so that they can bring healing to others.

“He has sent Me to heal the brokenhearted” Luke 4:18

Our Mission

We want to equip over a million people with the knowledge of how to bring healing to themselves and others.

“My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge” Hosea 4:6

Our Motivation

We want to fight for the real you, the one who God designed you to be, because that is who Jesus died for.

“He was beaten so we could be whole. He was whipped so we could be healed." Isaiah 53:5
